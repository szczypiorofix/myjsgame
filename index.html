The main goal of this game is to drive as long as you can through a post-apocalyptic world.

Your car is your best friend. Take care of it, collect spare parts and scrap!

Watch out your resorces: food and water.

Fighting enemies can make your shields damaged.

Use spare parts to repair or upgrade your car.

Sometimes you spot a village. You can go in to refuel and repair your car, buy some resources and spend some time talking to the villagers.
